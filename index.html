About Me

My name is Beatrice and I am a Media Studies student at Hunter College with a minor in Asian American Studies. I am graduating this summer of 2018. I was supposed to graduate for the Spring semester, but due to my mother's death at the beginning of the year, everything in my life has been quite convoluted- but I'm happy to just make it through Hunter and actually graduate. The Emerging Media concentration at Hunter is quite diverse, and lets you sample many different types of classes, but the fields I'm most interested in are Graphic Design, Web Design and Game Design. My Asian American Studies minor has also gotten me interested in advocacy and to spread awareness about Asian American issues, especially of Filipino Americans, who are one of highest immigrant groups, and I hope I can use my media skills to bring light to these issues as a career. Otherwise, I hope to get into marketing or more into web development.


Outside of academics, I like to sleep and draw. My life goals are to learn how to drive a motorcycle and travel the entire world, and I hope my mom, free of her suffering, is able to see it with me.
